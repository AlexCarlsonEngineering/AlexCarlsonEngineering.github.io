Combat Robotics

As the Mechanical Design Lead for Texas Combat Robotics, I spearheaded the design and modeling of UT's first Dogeweight (15lb) combat robot using SolidWorks. My role involved applying engineering analysis and Design for Manufacturing (DFM) principles to create a robust and maintainable robot. We competed at the inaugural SXSW Battle Bots Metal Mayhem event, where we proudly finished second in our group. This experience honed my skills in rapid prototyping and teamwork in a competitive environment.
